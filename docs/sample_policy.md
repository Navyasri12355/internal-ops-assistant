# Company Handbook — Sample Policies

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Full remote arrangements require VP approval and are reviewed quarterly. All remote employees must be available during core hours (10am–4pm IST) and attend weekly team syncs.

## Onboarding Process
New engineers complete a 2-week onboarding program. Week 1 covers tool setup, codebase walkthrough, and security training. Week 2 includes pairing with a buddy engineer and completing a starter task. The onboarding checklist is maintained in Notion.

## Leave Policy
Employees receive 24 days of paid leave per year. Leave must be requested at least 5 business days in advance via the HR portal. Carry-forward is limited to 10 days per calendar year.

## Production Access
Production database access is granted on a need-to-know basis. Requests must be submitted via the #infra-access Slack channel with your manager's approval. Access is reviewed and revoked after 90 days if unused.

## Equity & Vesting
Standard equity grant vests over 4 years with a 1-year cliff. The cliff date is calculated from your start date. Vesting accelerates to 100% on a qualifying acquisition event. Details are in your offer letter and ESOP documentation.

## Engineering Tools
The engineering team uses: GitHub for version control, Linear for issue tracking, Notion for documentation, Datadog for observability, and AWS for infrastructure. All engineers receive a $200/month tool stipend.
