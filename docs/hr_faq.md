# HR FAQ — People Operations

## Compensation & Payroll

**Q: When is payroll processed?**  
Payroll is processed on the last working day of each month. Salary is credited to your registered bank account within 2 business days.

**Q: How do I update my bank account details?**  
Submit a request via the HR portal under *My Profile → Payment Details*. Changes take effect the following payroll cycle.

**Q: Is there a performance bonus?**  
Yes. Eligible employees receive a discretionary annual bonus reviewed in Q1 each year. The bonus range is 10–25% of base salary depending on individual and company performance ratings.

---

## Benefits

**Q: What health insurance is offered?**  
The company provides comprehensive health insurance for the employee and up to 2 dependents. Coverage includes OPD, IPD, dental, and vision. Insurance details and network hospitals are in the benefits portal.

**Q: Is there a wellness allowance?**  
Employees receive a ₹5,000/month wellness allowance reimbursable for gym memberships, mental health subscriptions, or fitness equipment. Submit receipts via the HR portal within 30 days of purchase.

**Q: Does the company offer parental leave?**  
Primary caregivers receive 26 weeks of fully paid parental leave. Secondary caregivers receive 4 weeks. Leave can be taken continuously or in two split periods within the child's first year.

## Learning & Development

**Q: Is there a learning budget?**  
Yes. Each employee receives ₹30,000 per year for courses, certifications, conferences, and books. Budget resets on April 1. Unused budget does not carry forward.

**Q: How do I request L&D funding?**  
Submit a request via the HR portal under *Learning → New Request* with the course details and cost. Manager approval is required for amounts above ₹10,000.

**Q: Does the company support certifications?**  
Yes. The company will reimburse up to 2 professional certifications per year. Engineering certifications (AWS, GCP, CKA, etc.) are pre-approved. Others need manager sign-off.

## Performance Reviews

**Q: How often are performance reviews held?**  
Reviews are held twice a year: mid-year in July and annual in January. New employees have a 90-day review at the end of their probation period.

**Q: What is the rating scale?**  
Performance is rated on a 4-point scale: Exceptional, Meets Expectations, Needs Improvement, Unsatisfactory. Ratings feed into bonus and promotion decisions.

**Q: How do promotions work?**  
Promotion recommendations are made by managers during the annual review cycle. All promotions above Senior level require VP approval and are announced in Q2.

---

## Separation & Offboarding

**Q: What is the notice period?**  
The standard notice period is 60 days for all full-time employees. The company may waive part of the notice period at its discretion.

**Q: How is final settlement calculated?**  
Final settlement includes: remaining salary, encashment of unused leave (up to 30 days), gratuity (if eligible), and any pending expense reimbursements. Settlement is processed within 45 days of your last working day.

**Q: Do I keep my laptop?**  
Company equipment must be returned on or before your last working day. You will be sent a pre-paid courier label. Unreturned equipment is deducted from final settlement at depreciated value.
